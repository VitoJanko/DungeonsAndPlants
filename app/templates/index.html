Once per day, you may search for a plant: draw one card from the deck.

Every plant has a proficiency rating and can live in one or more habitats. You can only get plant cards that have a proficiency rating equal or lower to your herbalism proficiency and only those that can grow in your current habitat.

You cannot have more plants than your herbalism proficiency (or minimum of 1). The herbalist can give any plant to any other creature in their group, but it still counts towards his hand size until used (the herbalist is still caring for the plant).

Most cards have two modes of possible use. The first one usually gives an immediate effect, while the other one needs preparation time, equipment and/or expensive materials. The materials can be bought in any alchemy shop for the listed price. Unless stated otherwise, the plant is consumed by the use of either mode. The time required for different preparation procedures is listed below, but might be overruled by the text on card.

Consumed: One action.
Applied on skin or weapon: 10 minutes.
Planted: One hour.
Inhaled: One action for the creature that is carrying the plant, passive effect for any other creature to which the effect might apply.
Prepared (Alchemy kit, Dried, cut, Carved, Trimmed, etc): One day. You need to expand one short rest to actively perform the task, but must wait until the end of a long rest until you can use it (it is passively drying for example). If the card states that the procedure is longer, the short rest must be used each day. Only one plant can be prepared any given day.

Events: Some plants trigger an event when you try to collect them. If you successfully resolve the event, press the "check" button in the upper left corner. If you fail to do so (e.g., you flee the encounter or cannot reach the plant) discard the card.
